January 7, 1947
San Pedro<\/p>\n

Police have arrested eight youngsters on charges of burglary related to the brazen theft of weaponry from the Fort McArthur armory. The boys sawed through a lock and entered the building where returning troops’ weapons were stored, making off with a veritable arsenal of a dozen automatic pistols, four carbines plus jungle knives, bayonets and ammo.<\/p>\n

The thieves were discovered when they returned to the area to dig up their plunder, which they had hidden for later pick up.
