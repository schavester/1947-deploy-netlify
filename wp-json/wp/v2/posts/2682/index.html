July 6, 1947
Los Angeles<\/p>\n

It was a black day for Dingbat, long-haired tabby cat companion of Mr. and Mrs. Richard Patton of 571 S. Coronado Street<\/a>. The Pattons found a weird black bird out on the lawn, its feet tied with a leather thong, and, with typical human foolishness, thought it would be a good idea to bring the hellish creature into the apartment.<\/p>\n

The raven (or mynah, reports differ) promptly chased Dingbat out the door, and if it weren’t bad enough that every kid and cat and bird in the neighborhood knew of Dingbat’s shame, the man from the newspaper came and made kitty pose with the fowl interloper.<\/p>\n

Dingbat won’t be letting his owners out again for a long<\/span> time.<\/p>\n
