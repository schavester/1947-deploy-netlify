February 2, 1947
Los Angeles<\/p>\n

The Better Business Bureau has issued a public service warning about three bold swindles perpetrated against city residents in the past week.<\/p>\n

BEWARE: the door-to-door baby shoe bronzer, who will disappear with mama’s deposit and baby’s footwear, never to be seen again…<\/p>\n

DON’T PAY: the frantic “exterminator” who rushes into a business with a sack of “the DDT the boss ordered,” demands prompt payment, and flees, leaving his hapless victim with a sack of ordinary flour…<\/p>\n

AND TURN AWAY: the seller of off-brand vacuum cleaners, which are often not just unfamilar, but second-hand and overpriced.
