Get miffy at tennis pro hubby when a lady tennis player calls to suggest they team up in a tournament match, cry as he splits for the L.A. Cahuenga.<\/a> BTW, fellas, it’s a big<\/span> red flag when your sweetie doesn’t just try to poison herself, but administers her own antidote!<\/p>\n
