June 20, 1947
Bellflower<\/p>\n

Mayonnaise is rotten stuff, as the parents of little Steven Barrett, 4, discovered after their son was killed today when a car driven by Victor Martinez, 22, of 4321 Elm St., Long Beach, jumped the curb and crushed the tyke as he played on the lawn in front of his home at 5833 Penswood Ave.<\/a> Martinez reported that he had lost control when a jar of mayo rolled off his bench seat, and he lunged for it.
