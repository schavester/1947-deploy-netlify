July 17, 1927<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/p>\n

In an effort to redeem a luscious dairy treat\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s good name after being caught in a bootlegging scandal<\/a> last week\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009d-or perhaps because it was a slow news day\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009d-Dr. Frank McCoy today announced that \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cICE CREAM IS A REAL FOOD.\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d <\/p>\n

This is the good news we\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2ve been waiting for, folks. According to Dr. McCoy (author of the Los Angeles Times<\/i>\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cHealth and Diet Advice\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d column), ice cream \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cshould at all times be considered a real food and not a delicacy.\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d Besides being rich in vitamin A and calcium, a half-pint of ice cream has as much lime as a half-pound of butter, four pounds of meat, or three-and-a-half pounds of potatoes. Never heard about the importance of lime in your diet? Me either\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009d-but if we eat enough ice cream, we\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2ll never have to worry about it again. <\/p>\n

Dr. McCoy also has a word or two for those who criticize manufacturers for adding gelatin to their ice creams. \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201c\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u00a6 I would suggest that the laws be changed to admit the use of even more gelatin, as this is an excellent food product which makes the ice cream still more palatable and delicious.\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d <\/p>\n

Packed with all that lime and gelatin, ice cream is a veritable superfood. Dr. McCoy \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201csuggest[s] that you try some summer lunches with ice cream as the principal part of the meal, using with it any one kind of the acid fruits \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u00a6 or \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u00a6 cooked and raw nonstarchy vegetables.\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d Ice cream salad, anyone? <\/p>\n

Should you find your pants getting a little tight after all that healthfulness, you might want to take a look at Dr. McCoy\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s book, The Fast Way to Health<\/i>.\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
