Check out those rounded corner windows. I\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2m guessing 1938-40 on this one. I know I should go down and pull records, but while ars longa, you know, vita brevis. <\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Slight eaves with dentils–and those full-height pilasters–give this a Classical touch, but the sort of faux corner tower and arched dormer makes me itch with French Eclectic. Bet Solly boy died on hardwood floors near a nicely tiled bathroom. Now I’m itching to get in a take a look-see.<\/p>\n
