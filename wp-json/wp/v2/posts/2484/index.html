The 1928 Schulte United building has been downgraded to a \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201chut.\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d A shoe hut, no less.<\/p>\n

Downtown strollers beware, indeed. Vanishing money is endemic to the area. It\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s rife with grifters and dips parting citizens from their geetus. Half a block down from Schulte I espied this collection of folk.
<\/p>\n

And what were they entranced by? Why it\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s that old street con, the shell game!
<\/p>\n

And Mr. Flimmflam-man had no lack of marks today. The Deep Pinch, the V-Grip, Side Steal, Inside Shift and Side Load; sharpie had it down when spieling the nuts. <\/p>\n

There are times I wish I\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2d been sober during my high school Spanish classes; woulda loved to glom said spiel, which had that unmistakable rhythm of Three Card Monte patter.<\/p><\/div>\n<\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
