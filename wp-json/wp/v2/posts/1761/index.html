\nLos Angeles<\/p>\n

<\/o:p><\/p>\n

Architect Charles Mulford Robinson<\/a> has drafted a proposal for downtown Los Angeles that is stunning in its ambition. One portion calls for broad boulevard leading from a proposed Union Station at Central and 5th<\/sup> Street toward Grand, ending at a new public library and art gallery. The other, equally elaborate, calls for a grouping of civic buildings and terraced gardens around North Spring Street, including a new City Hall. <\/p>\n
