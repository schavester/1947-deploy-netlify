\nMarch 2, 1927
\nLos Angeles<\/p>\n

The Wonderly shooting of Emerson over an elephant’s affection has been continued until March 15th.<\/p>\n

George \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cScotty\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d Wonderly is the keeper of the bloodhounds for the studios, George Emerson, trainer of the lions at Universal City.<\/p>\n

The story has a Old West theme to it to boot: on 15 February last, the two were arguing over the affections of said elephant at Universal. Wonderly called Emerson out; Wonderly strapped on his single shot .45 and there, on a rain-soaked muddy street, surrounded by wooden shacks and gambling dens and dance halls, Emerson staggered and twisted and slowly fell, a bullet through his breast, out his back, and lodged into the Last Chance Saloon. Elephants and the Old West, together again.\n<\/p>\n

\n\n<\/p>\n

\nHere, Wonderly shows how his beloved raised its trunk…and went on to maintain that he acted in self-defense.<\/p>\n

And of the love that dare not trumpet its name…the true heart\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s desire of the elephant remains a sweet mystery.\n<\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
