February 27, 1947
near Long Beach<\/p>\n

Fiends are loose in the city, and no girl or woman is safe! The latest victim is a 17-year-old high school girl–found naked and bound with her own underclothes, with a cigarette burn on her wrist–near the Union Pacific underpass at Willow Street. After treatment at Seaside Hospital, which revealed no sign of criminal assault, the shaken girl recovered sufficiently to describe being followed by a strange man and then grabbed in an alley, after which her memory was mercifully blank.<\/p>\n

Police records show the same girl reported being followed by a man in a car with no license plates on February 5.<\/p><\/div>\n<\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
