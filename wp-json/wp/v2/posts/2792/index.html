<\/p>\n

Hey, says your buddy. Book joint out in City Terrace. Wire room and everything, whole system of runners, jobbies and sheeties. So you call a comeback to lay a trey to show parlay on a high-stepper. Beats having to actually go out to City Terrace\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009dthat place gives you the heebie-jeebies, what with all the signs in Yiddish and all.<\/p>\n

Evidence of the area\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s Hebraic genealogy is now gone, as is the upholstery shop. (Also removed is the Pacific Electric line a block north, replaced by the 10; the traincars once headed to the mighty PE rail yard, where today stands Cal State LA.)
