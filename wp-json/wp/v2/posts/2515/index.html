So let\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s talk about the sandwich for a moment, that legacy of John Montagu, Fourth Earl of Sandwich, famously corrupt and inept Lord of the Royal Navy, and inveterate gambler who needed something quick to eat at the card table. The\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u00a6you know. There\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s just something wrong with this guy. There\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s still a godforsaken island chain named after him that neither the Brits nor Argentines wanted in the Falkland War and kept insisting belonged to the other fellow.<\/p>\n

And his namesake, well, it\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s just evil. Consider our manwich post of 3 October. A battalion commander\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009dguy who probably knows his way around a sword and stuff\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009dguts himself in one of those sandwich-making-gone-horribly-wrong accidents. To recap:<\/p>\n

The inside of Cmdr. George A. Tucker:

That pink thing, that\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s his stomach. The little purple doodad to its right, that\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s a spleen. I could see how one could stab both while making a ham sandwich. Common enough. But to get up under the sternum and get to the heart? That’s Montagu\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s hand stabbing from the heart of hell, it is. <\/p>\n

And now, some witch, using sandwitchery, has bedeviled poor Raymond Adame. The likely final destination of his abduction of the pernicious Celina was to her coven at Wilson Sandwich…

\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009dby \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cmodern equipment\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d they mean hi-tech new streamlined cauldrons\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009dfor only there could they release the spell. Or maybe he was off to stone her, as per Leviticus 20:27. <\/p>\n

He attempted to abduct her from here:

<\/p>\n
