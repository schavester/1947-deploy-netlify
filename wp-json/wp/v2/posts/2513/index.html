November 7, 1947
Glendale<\/p>\n

Waitress Betty Fox, 23, currently sharing digs at 1248 S. Boyington Ave., really needs an apartment–she can’t get married until she finds a place for she and hubby to canoodle. The grapevine hummed with news of a three-room flat a soldier was giving up as he entered service, so she tracked the new recruit down and asked if his place might still be available.<\/p>\n

“Sure thing, toots, and if you gimme 48 bucks that’ll cover the first month’s rent.”<\/p>\n

She ponied up in exchange for the key and address, and soon learned that there is no such number.<\/p><\/div>\n<\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
