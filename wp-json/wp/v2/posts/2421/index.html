And who among us is surprised to see…another nurse.<\/p>\n

Yes, I love their embittered hearts and unrepentant drug addiction and that I love their overblown need for role playing is an unfortunate matter of public record, but their fundamental incapacity to discern between “alive” and “dead,\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d that\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s the part of nursing that weirds me out.<\/p>\n

But Ralford Jr. rose\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u00a6like Lazarus, with Nurse Alma\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s bidding? Or did the li\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2l revenant self-resurrect, rolling his rock away from the entrance of the morgue? In any event, pointless theological speculation aside, Junior up and dies again, making him some sort of undead I Am Legend<\/span> spawn.<\/p>\n

All in all, the question remains, what\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s a dead baby worth? Above and beyond the nine months of minimum wage that is your due? The Ralfords asked for 100k and settled for five. Telling. Of course, kid would have grown up to be one of those self obsessed boomer types. Ahem. <\/p>\n

This much we know with certainty: screams from a morgue\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009dthere\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s a sound you won’t soon forget. <\/p>\n
