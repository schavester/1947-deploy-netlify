Jan. 16, 2007<\/p>\n

Sfxarchive<\/a> is selling a 4- by 5-inch negative ostensibly from the Black Dahlia case. I don\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2t recognize any of these individuals, nor have I ever seen the image. It doesn\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2t show any of the main suspects, nor does it show the main detectives, Harry Hansen and Finis Brown. <\/p>\n

I would say its importance is limited. Note that the seller\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s date (January 1949) is contradicted by the calendar in the picture, which says December 1948.<\/p>\n

The bottom line: I would not spend any serious money on this image. <\/p>\n
