The Dorsey home on Romaine is no more. Whatever it was, it probably soaked up too much spatter to ever be truly clean. In its place:<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

A sort of dingbat, though not really. Dingbattian in its boxy, stucco\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2d, cheap construction\/maximum density elements, but lacking covered parking between the stilts, and the all-essential facade-affixed architectural eccentricity (interlocking boxes, backlit asterisks, abstracted fish, some anamorphic squiggles) that forced Francis Ventre to spit at Reyner Banham while they were tooling around LA: \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cMy God<\/em> that\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s dingbat architecture!\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d<\/p>\n

The address font is rather somber, although an attempt was made to apply it jauntily. It\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s no \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cShangri-Lodge\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d or \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cSeahorse Arms\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d in giant fanciful script, but then, something so lacking in sobriety would likely disturb and spur the Dorsey\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s ghosts into demoniacal bloodlust.
