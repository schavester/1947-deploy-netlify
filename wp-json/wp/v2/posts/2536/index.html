October 20, 1947
Newhall<\/p>\n

After being forced off the road around 3 am, 64-year-old rancher Heinie Rodemacher spent twelve hours pinned beneath his pick-up truck in a dry wash off Highway 99 until his cries were heard. Gasoline and battery acid dripped onto him during his ordeal.<\/p>\n

The fatalistic fellow told attending CHP officer Joe Green and Dep. Sheriffs W.C. Collin and L.C. Smith “you’ll never get me out of here,” but two hours of hacksaw and acetylene torch work did the trick.<\/p>\n

Rodemacher is at Newhall Community Hospital for treatment of his chemical burns and leg injuries.<\/p><\/div>\n<\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
