June 28, 1947
Arroyo Seco<\/p>\n

Two Pasadena men died tonight when the truck in which they were driving skidded sixty feet, crashed through a guard rail, and plunged 300 feet down the Devil\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s Gate Dam bridge near the Rose Bowl.<\/p>\n

Driver Ernest Jimenez, 28, of 1993 Linda Rosa Ave. and George Talbot, 32, of 1143 Mentone St. were both dead by the time Ambulance Surgeon Charles A. Wagner and Driver Jack Bradley climbed down to attend them. Firemen cut the victims from the truck cab with torches, and the bodies were lifted to the road in wire baskets.<\/p>\n
