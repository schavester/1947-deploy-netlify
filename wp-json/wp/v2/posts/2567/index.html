People everywhere stop me on the street and query \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cWhat up dog, where you been?\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d I consider this, wonder why they\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2re talking like that, and go on to tell them that while I\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2ve been away, I’ve now returned and am, I add, in full effect. <\/p>\n

Seems I went off and did this<\/a> -\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u0153 (I\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2m not only a first-class cameraman, but can portray Karl Benz in a Velomobile with the best of them) — and simply neglected to cease traveling. Lot of crime scenes in Cuzco and Sumer to be investigated after all. Sadly, my photos of Atlantean gang killings were \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201clost\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d by the lab. Damn Illuminati.<\/p>\n

Anyway, I\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2d left McCarthy at the dock, so trusty Packard and I immediately set forth, traversing town to catch up on old posts. Which are now live\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u20ac\u009dwitness twenty-five brand-spanking-new<\/em> entries au go-go,five for July, eleven in August, another nine in September. <\/p>\n

Love and kisses, and death to all those who oppose us, <\/p>\n
