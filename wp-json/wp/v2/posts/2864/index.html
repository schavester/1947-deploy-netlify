Whoever stucco’d this house should have their hands cut off. I don’t say that to be funny or cute. Stucco, aluminum windows, crappy gates… I hope you’re all happy. And handless.<\/p>\n

I’m certain terrible things happen in this house all the time. But we’re not here to postulate such.<\/p>\n

We’ve got 1947 to bring back.<\/p>\n

Imagine the smell of cordite hanging in the air. The aroma of electrical cord against flesh. Bits of sofa wafting about along with these scents.<\/p>\n
