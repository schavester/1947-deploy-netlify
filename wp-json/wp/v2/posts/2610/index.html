Aye, wee Ruthie McLaren, a gal after my own heart. Any fifteen year-old with a penchant for thievery, and men met in movie houses, quickens the heart of this tender lad. Sure, she might have been better off at the College of Ave Maria than at Mary\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s Beauty College, but with her autodidactic doctorate in Acquiring Fur Coats, apparently she doesn\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2t need either institution.<\/p>\n

Hard to tell from where she hailed, as there\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s no 23\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u00a6the house numbers go from 19 to 25:<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n
