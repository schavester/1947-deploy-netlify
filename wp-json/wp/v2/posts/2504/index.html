November 13, 1947
Los Angeles<\/p>\n

17-year-old Ray Luedeman was cleaning rags in a pan of gasoline on the back porch of his home at 1877 W. 38th Street<\/a> when the automatic gas heater beside him clicked on. Suddenly realizing the danger of the gas igniting, he scooped up the pan, sending gas flying all over the heater and his clothing. He’s in Georgia Street Receiving Hospital being treated for serious burns.<\/p>\n
