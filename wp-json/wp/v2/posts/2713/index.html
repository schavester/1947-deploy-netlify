June 15, 1947
China City<\/a>
<\/span>
Pity Wanzy Patterson of 1312 W. 37th Street<\/a>. The night watchman, 29, somehow came to the conclusion that his job demanded he shimmy over the transom of the bar at 777 Quang Yn Court<\/a> and relieve the establishment of some of that pesky joy juice. This had been going on for some time, so officers C.A. Stromwell and M. Herman secreted themselves within the establishment, guns aready for when the liquor bandit made a repeat appearance.<\/p>\n

Soon enough ol’ Wanzy dropped in, and laid his paws upon several handy bottles. The cops revealed themselves and Wanzy moved as if to retrieve a gun. Bang! 11 shots pierced the hapless Wanzy, and more’n likely the bottles he so loved.<\/p><\/div>\n<\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
