December 2, 1947
Watts<\/p>\n

Police at the 77th Street Station are wrapping up a two-day sweep of neighborhood nogoodniks, having dragged 31 suspected robbers (male) and 8 grand theft person suspects (female) out of bars at 10218 and 10224 Graham Ave<\/a>. A number of those arrested were armed with knives. No additional details were provided.<\/p>\n
