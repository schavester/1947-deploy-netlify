A message from our pal Jonathon at the essential L.A. Time Machines<\/a> site, who is speaking at this March 18 event:<\/p>\n
The group we are forming will focus on Los Angeles Restaurant History in general, with an emphasis on restaurant memorabilia collectors, writers on restaurant history and the oral history of classic restaurant employees and owners. Scheduled to speak at our first meeting are Jim Heimann<\/span>, author of “Out With the Stars”<\/a> and “May I Take Your Order”<\/a>, as well as countless other books on Los Angeles<\/a> and Pop Culture<\/a> of the 30s to 70s. Also Charles Perry<\/span>, the renown columnist for the Los Angeles Times and Food Historian. And Filomena D’Amore<\/span>, owner of Patsy’s Pizza, whose father Patsy D’Amore owned the Villa Capri restaurant in Hollywood along with Frank Sinatra (and it was James Dean’s favorite place). Our first meeting is at the Hollywood Heritage Museum on March 18 at 10AM.<\/span><\/div>\n
<\/div>\n
Most of this is on the attachment hopefully. The principal organizer is Chris Nichols who you might know from ModCom or LA magazine.<\/span><\/div>\n
