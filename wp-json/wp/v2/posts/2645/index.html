August 1, 1947
Sacramento<\/p>\n

The state is growing, but it’s becoming less thirsty–or so it appears from a State Board of Equalization report issued today, which pegs the May 1946 liquor consumtion rate at 1,891,669 gallons of the hard stuff, May 1947 a paltry 1,192,228. The liquor industry is nervously suggesting a return to the 25\u00c3\u201a\u00c2\u00a2 cocktail…. but mayhap the numbers aren’t what they seem to be.
