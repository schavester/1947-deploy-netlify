May 6, 1927
Coolidge America <\/p>\n

Let’s keep abreast of the rising waters, shall we?  The men in top-hats and diamond stick-pins are upset that cotton and cotton related goods are on the downswing, and the decline of trade\/farmwork\/rail freight is destroying the country, and the dollar is weakening.<\/p>\n

Of course, the less elaborately garbed cannot muster quite the concern: <\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

But enterprising folk around Memphis have begun erecting tree houses in the great cottonwoods and willows, where they build their stills to keep a thirsty populace sated.  Sheriff Knight has seized a dozen giant hooch-hatcheries from the treetops, placed there by ingenious bootleggers.<\/p>\n

Here in the southland, the Orange County Fruit Exchange began sending citrus, and the stars came out to raise 125g’s (1,475,000 USD2005).<\/p>\n
