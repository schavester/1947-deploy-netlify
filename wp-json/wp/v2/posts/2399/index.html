January 12, 1947
Long Beach<\/p>\n

Driving home to 1572 W. Seventh Street with his wife, Frank R. Cross heard a weird rattle in the car.<\/p>\n

“Honey,” said Frank, “You take the wheel, and I’m going to just hop up on the bumper and see if I can figure out what’s going on.”<\/p>\n

“But Frank, I can’t! You know I haven’t driven a car in eight years!”<\/p>\n

Frank insisted, and so they stopped, swapped, and hubby hopped up on the bumper… and promptly disappeared. Mrs. Cross heard a bump, but not a rattle, and drove on for a spell. Then she wondered if the bump might have been Frank, so she stopped–fortunately, since the bump named Frank was being dragged beneath her wheels.<\/p>\n

Mr. Cross is in Seaside Hospital tonight with multiple injuries, his condition listed as satisfactory.<\/p><\/div>\n<\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
