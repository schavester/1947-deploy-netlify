October 30, 1947
Pomona<\/p>\n

Still catching up on his jet lag following a Congressional fact-finding tour of post war Europe, Representative Richard M. Nixon is beaming after receiving a Good Government Award from the Junior Chamber of Commerce at a dinner in his honor last week.<\/p>\n

At a luncheon meeting in Whittier earlier that day, Nixon quipped, “If we lose the battle for democracy in Europe, we can consider it lost at home.”<\/p>\n
