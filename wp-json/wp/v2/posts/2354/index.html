February 4, 1947
Culver City<\/p>\n

Miss Isabel Foster feels lucky to be alive tonight after being accosted by a man in a 1940 sedan while she was waiting for a bus at West Adams and Redondo Blvd. He asked if she needed a ride, and when she demurred showed a butcher knife and demanded she climb into the passenger seat. Terrified, she complied.<\/p>\n

“Where’dya live?”
“3-3-3-3895 M-m-m-main Street<\/a>.”
“Stop blubbering!”
“I… can’t…”
“Shut up, or I’ll give you what I gave the Black Dahlia!” And with that he cut her across the knuckles. The man drove closer to Isabel’s home. When they were a block away, she opened the passenger door and ran.<\/p>\n

Her attacker is described as, slim, dark skinned, late 20s, about 5’8″, wearing dark work clothes and a “ridiculous” stocking cap.<\/p>\n
