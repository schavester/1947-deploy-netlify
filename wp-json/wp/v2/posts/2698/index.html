June 25, 1947
Hollywood<\/p>\n

If you thought the blockades<\/a> were over, think again. Tonight, 250 of the city\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s finest converged on Hollywood in a night-long rampage to break the back of vice.
