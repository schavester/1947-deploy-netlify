December 14, 1947
Atwater Village<\/p>\n

When Mrs. Evelyn Schott got off the street car near her home at 3224 Garden Ave.<\/a>, she stepped unknowingly into a trap. For lurking one block from home’s safety was a man with evil intent. He sprung upon Evelyn from behind a bush and commenced beating her head. She screamed, neighbors poured onto their lawns, and her assailant jumped into a car and split. Evelyn was patched up at Pasadena Ave. Emergency Hospital, and won’t, we wager, be walking home alone after dark again anytime soon.
