December 20, 1947
Hollywood<\/p>\n

Shoppers in the S.H. Kress Co.<\/a> store at 6608 Hollywood Boulevard<\/a> this evening were terrorized when a gun-wielding robber forced floorman John W. Cossett to empty a register and hand over $110 before fleeing the scene.<\/p>\n
