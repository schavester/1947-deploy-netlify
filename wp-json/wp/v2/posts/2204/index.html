\u00c2\u00a0<\/p>\n

December 16, 1927
\nSawtelle<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Los Angeles Police Captain W. L. Hagenbaugh feeds more juice into the stills of Sawtelle than he gets from them; after he raids the moonshiners and chops up their contraptions of copper and coil, he fashions fixtures and floor lamps for his new nine-room Spanish job up on Comstock in Westwood.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

Recently, materials from three forty gallon bootleg stills, lined in some very fine silver, have been reclaimed from their sinful ways and turned toward this honest enterprise.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

This writer\u00e2\u20ac\u2122s inquisitive interests now satisfied\u00e2\u20ac\u201dyeah, you\u00e2\u20ac\u2122re green<\/em>, I get it\u00e2\u20ac\u201dmy acquisitive interest takes over:\u00c2\u00a0 where are these shades now<\/em>?<\/p>\n
