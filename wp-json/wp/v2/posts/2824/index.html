 \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00cb\u0153Course, sometimes LA is just about a vegetable-crazy guy named Trinidad what took his rifle wading into the brack of Lincoln Park lake. Was his offering to be accepted as Abel\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s? Or was he just itching to glaze the fowl bastard in honey and lavender, to feel the essence of something once-sentient poured down his throat in the form of pan juices?<\/p>\n
We shall not know.  Those in the throes of Vegetable Frenzy are beyond comprehension.<\/p>\n
