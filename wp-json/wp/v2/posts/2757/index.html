In their size and shape and arrangement, the houses along Marine all oozed prewar. But then, they all looked new as well. I chatted up a neighbor. Yes, her house was built in the thirties, the whole street was. Really, I said. There have been some\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u00a6changes. Oh yes, she replied, we did ours in \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a286. The people across the street there just did theirs.<\/p>\n

So. Stucco and plastic windows are something you \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cdo.\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d You just call someone up and say \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cI\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2d like to do<\/em> my house,\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d and they\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2ll know exactly what you mean. They\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2ll come over and your house will get \u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c5\u201cdid.\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00c2\u009d Nearly every visible trace of 30s Wilmington, borne of the 1932-discovered 2.5 billion barrel Wilmington Field (third largest in America), has been wiped from the landscape.<\/p>\n

As to what Betty\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s outfit signifies we can only conjecture; what types of costume (and their import) hung in the closets of 1610 is a matter of further fantasy. How had Mrs. Foley puzzled out her daughter\u00c3\u00a2\u00e2\u201a\u00ac\u00e2\u201e\u00a2s dangerous games? Dear Myrtle must have filled her head with ignis fatuus to dispel any disquieting truths.
