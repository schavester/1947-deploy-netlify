January 19, 1927
Santa Monica, Venice<\/p>\n

A drained, shamefaced whisky bottle and wrecked car were all officers found tonight at Colorado Blvd and Twenty-Third Street<\/a>.<\/p>\n

A thorough check of the hospitals and morgues revealed nothing further.<\/p>\n

In nearby Venice, at Washington and Brooks<\/a>, an ambulance was summoned when excited folk in the vicinity witnessed an auto turn turtle.  In true 1920s fashion, the two young male occupants righted the thing and drove off, presumably in a crazy zigzag with zany piano accompaniment.  <\/p>\n<\/p><\/div>\n<\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
