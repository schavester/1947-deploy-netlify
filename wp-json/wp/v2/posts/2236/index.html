January 19, 1927
Santa Monica, Venice<\/p>\n

A drained, shamefaced whisky bottle and wrecked car were all officers found tonight at Colorado Blvd and Twenty-Third Street<\/a>.<\/p>\n

A thorough check of the hospitals and morgues revealed nothing further.<\/p>\n
